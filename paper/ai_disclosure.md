# AI-Assistance Disclosure

Most venues now ask for this; stating it plainly protects credibility.

> The author used AI-based tools (large language model coding and writing
> assistants) to scaffold the analysis code, to draft and edit prose, and to
> organize references. All study design choices, the feature and model selection,
> the execution and checking of the analysis, the interpretation of results, and
> the final wording were made and verified by the author. The author takes full
> responsibility for the accuracy and integrity of the work. No AI tool is listed
> as an author.

Adapt to the venue's exact policy. What you must be able to back up: that the
design, interpretation, and claims are genuinely yours. See `defense_brief.md`.
